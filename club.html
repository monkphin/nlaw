N/L/A/W

Newton Local Area Wargamers

We currently have 10 full sized tables measuring 6'x4' along with a large collection of themed mats and painted scenery to allow for a wide veriety of settings and systems to be supported. We're always looking to expand our inventory and run a volunteer provided 3d printer farm to help with this.
We also have a broad selection of board games and can arrange for demonstrations on request.
If you have never participated in tabletop gaming or RPGs before and would like to learn more, please get in touch, we would be happy to answer any questions you have and arrange for a drop in session to help you learn more.

Onsite we have various facilities for our members, providing drinks, both hot and cold as well as snacks, this is offered on an honour system, where we trust our members to pay for what is consumed but don't hold them to this. Members are also allowed tp bring their own food and drink to the premesis. We ask a monthly subscription of £10 for membership to cover the costs of upkeep and running the venue, as well as purchasing more items for use by the membership. We do allow for people to use the club without being members for a fee of £10 per occaision, this will be taken as your monthly fee if you choose to join us.

Club Rules

  1. Club fees should be paid, or a valid membership card shown, on arrival.
  2. All visitors must sign in on arrival and sign out on departure.
  3. Please place all rubbish in the provided bins and keep the club tidy.
  4. Keep all bags/cases clear of the aisles to prevent tripping.
  5. No smoking or vaping in the club or wider centre.
  6. Drugs or Alcohol of any kind are not permitted at any time.
  7. Bullying or abusive behaviour will not be tolerated.
  8. N/L/A/W is a family environment with a policy of inclusivity, we would ask you to respect that and act appropriately.
  9. Terrain at each table may be arranged as required but please don’t remove terrain or mats from designated tables without asking the club volunteer on duty.
  10. If you accidentally damage terrain, or spot damaged terrain, let the volunteer on duty know so we can get it fixed.
  11. Gamers who don’t follow these rules will be reminded of their duty to do so.
  12. Gamers who receive repeated warnings may be suspended or banned from attending the club.

The club is typically open 3 nights a week, Monday to Friday and every weekend. Though we can open the club outside these times if given notice.
