N/L/A/W

Newton Local Area Wargamers

N/L/A/W came about from a recognition that getting together around a table to play games can have a huge range of social benefits. It was first founded in 2022 as part of Ascent Collages efforts to expand on its students education beyond just the basic curriculum, however after changes made to how DofE clubs could operate and adjustments to the collages operating times caused us to have to stop hosting on campus meetings we decided to form a dedicated hobby club, since its members felt they were beneffiting greatly from participating. The club felt that they all benefit in many ways from getting together weekly and wanted to not only continue this, but increase their interacctions as well as share those benefits with the wider community.

Tabletop gaming and roleplaying games have many benefits to those who participate. Ranging from social interactions when actually playing games, mindfullness benefits when working on the more creative hobby aspects, such as painting minis or crafting scenery, helping stretch your creative legs via painting minis or writing stories and background for them. The games themselves can help with basic matchs skills, due to the need to add up in game costs of armies to ensure a rough balance of armies ont he tabletop, to calculating outcomes of dice roles and gauging percetange chances when playing.

We're a not for profit organisation run and staffed by volunteers who historically helped run clubs at the collage. The club has a relaxed atmophere, with a mix of people from 16 and upwards who enjoy playing a veriety of tabletop games, ranging from wargames; such as Warhammer 40,000, RPG's such as Dungeons and Dragons and more classic boardgames. Though at our heart we're more focused on RPGs and tabletop wargaming. We aim to provide the local community of game enthusiasts to meet and collectivly enjoy their hobby together, we're always welcome to curious members of the community coming along and hopefully even returning to visit in the future.
